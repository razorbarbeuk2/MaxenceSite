About Us

Welcome the the Wild Circus!
I know you've heard of us but let us give you a little bit of history first. The Wild Circus was born in 1853 in Transilvania. His father Radu Todiras was a dreamer and a genius. He studied in the famous Engineering School of Russia and became one of the few to graduate. In this times there were revolutions all over Europe and Radu went back to his family in Romania. He was working for the Romanian academy center to build the first trains of the country. This place was the perfect playground for him. During his spare time his creativity never stopped. Since he heard about the Mongolfier brothers and their hot air balloon in France his greatest dream has been to fly into the sky.

A few years later Radu had already build a lot of curious machines in the atempt of flying, traveling faster, traveling in time. He could never stop building. He succesfully built is own hot air balloon, made different kind of glider aircrafts and what was the ancestor of the train in his country. Then came his greatest idea after an exhibition show "The Great Exhibition" in 1951 in London:
he would travel the world to show all his inventions to the curious, the young, the old and entertain them.

In 1953 the "Radu's Curious Machines" show was born and was the only one of its kind. 6 years later and after a wonderful and succesful trip over Europe the show was renamed "Wild Circus". Until today it has been held by Radu's family and people from all over the world want to see the curious machine.

Now it's your turn to enjoy the show!
You are looking for something different and an experience like no other?
You want to share Radu's dream and fantasies ?
Then this is for you!
